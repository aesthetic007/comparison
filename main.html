SPORTS BIKE



A Sports bike is a type of motorcycle which falls under the performance-bike segment. The manufacturers optimize the sports bikes for acceleration (pick-up), speed, braking, and cornering abilities. However, these performance parameters come at the cost of comfort and fuel economy compared to other motorcycles.



CRUISER BIKE



A CRUISER is a motorcycle in the style of American machines from the 1930s to the early 1960s, including those made by Harley-Davidson, Indian, Excelsior and Henderson. Cruisers with greater performance than usual, including more horsepower, stronger brakes and better suspension, are often called power cruisers.
